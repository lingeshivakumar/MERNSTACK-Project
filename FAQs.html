Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our dental services.

1. How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online through our website or call our front desk.

2. What dental services do you offer?

We provide general dentistry, orthodontics, teeth whitening, root canals, and more.

3. Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most dental insurance plans. Please check with our reception for details.

4. How much do treatments cost?

Prices vary depending on the treatment. Contact us for a consultation.

5. What should I do in case of a dental emergency?

Call our emergency number immediately or visit our clinic as soon as possible.

6. Is teeth whitening safe?

Yes, professional teeth whitening is safe and effective when done by a dentist.

7. How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend a check-up every six months for optimal dental health.

8. What age should my child have their first dental visit?

Your child should visit the dentist by their first birthday or when their first tooth appears.
